Biography

Dr. Xinwei Shen is an Associate Prof./Principle Investigator, who also serves as the Director of Office of Students’ Affairs, at Tsinghua SIGS. His research interests include power system / integrated energy system / offshore renewable energy optimization. He graduated from Dept. of Electrical Eng., Tsinghua Univ. with B. Eng. and Ph. D., and used to be a visiting scholar at IIT, U. C. Berkeley and Univ. of Macau. He led several NSFC/National Key R&D of China/industry-sponsored research projects. He is a senior member of IEEE and CES, the young editorial board member of CSEE JPES/Applied Energy, and technical program co-chair of IEEE PES Energy Internet Coordinating Committee. He is also the winner of IEEE PES Technical Council Young Professionals Award (in 2023, the first recipient in Asia-pacific region) and winner of “Young Elite Scientists Sponsorship Program” by CSEE, Best Paper Award at IEEE PES GM et al.
